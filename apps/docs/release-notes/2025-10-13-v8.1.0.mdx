## What's new

### Correct login configuration lookup

Individual accounts were incorrectly falling back to the team login configuration, even for users without a team. Individual accounts now use their own configuration, and team members continue to inherit their team's rules.

### Verify email via link

Fixed an issue where valid 2FA email-verification codes were sometimes rejected.
