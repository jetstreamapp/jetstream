## What's new

### Google export from Permissions Manager

When exporting permissions to Google Drive, Jetstream was occasionally picking CSV over Xlsx and triggering a local download instead of uploading to Google. The file-type order has been adjusted so Xlsx takes priority and the export lands in Google Drive as intended.
