## What's new

### SSO honors your identity provider's authentication rules

When your team signs in with SSO, Jetstream previously asked the identity provider for a specific
authentication context (the `requestedAuthnContext` in the SAML request). Some providers handle that
strictly and would reject otherwise-valid logins. Jetstream now leaves that decision to your identity
provider, so whatever sign-in method it enforces — multi-factor, conditional access, and so on — is
respected without Jetstream getting in the way. If your team logs in through SSO, sign-in should just
work more consistently.
