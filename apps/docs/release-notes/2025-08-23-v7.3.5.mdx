## What's new

### Diff editor

When comparing two pieces of metadata, swapping the left and right sides previously left the collapsed unchanged regions out of sync. Collapsing now works consistently after swapping. Reported in [#1315](https://github.com/jetstreamapp/jetstream/issues/1315).
