## What's new

### Keyboard navigation that gets out of your way

Getting around Jetstream with the keyboard is much smoother in this release. Popovers now properly take
focus when they open (previously they often didn't, which made tabbing through them frustrating), and
focus returns where you'd expect when they close. The org-info popover and the app tab bar also got
keyboard-navigation and focus fixes.

### Smoother drag-and-drop

The drag-and-drop used for organizing org groups and building query filters has been rebuilt on a modern,
actively-maintained library. Moving orgs between groups and rearranging filter rows feels more reliable
and responsive as a result.

### The browser extension stops churning tokens

The web extension's access token was expiring far more often than it should have, due to race conditions
and over-eager refreshing. That's been fixed, so the extension refreshes its token much less frequently
and stays connected more steadily.

### Other fixes

- A round of security hardening: tighter socket/proxy origin checks, stronger protection against TOTP
  reuse and account enumeration, improved at-rest encryption for tokens, and tightened content-security
  policies.
- Upgraded dependencies and improved logging and error reporting, including more robust copy-to-clipboard
  handling.
