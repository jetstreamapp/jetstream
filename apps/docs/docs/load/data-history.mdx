Jetstream keeps a local history of the data modifications you make, so you can always go back and
see exactly what you changed, when, and what the results were — including the loaded records and
per-record success/failure files.

History is captured for:

- **Load Records** (including retries and trial runs)
- **Load Records to Multiple Objects**
- **Update Records Without a File** and **Bulk Update From Query**
- **Query results edits** (inline table edits and bulk edits)
- **Record modal saves** (create, edit, clone) and **Create records without a file**

Open Data History from the **clock icon in the header**, the **Load Records menu**, or the home page.

## Your data stays on your device

:::info

Data History is stored **locally on this device** — in your browser's private storage (or the
desktop app's local storage). It is **never sent to the Jetstream server**.

This also means **clearing your browser's site data permanently deletes your history**. Download
anything you need to keep long-term.

:::

Jetstream automatically asks the browser for "persistent storage" to protect your history from
automatic cleanup — no action needed on your part.

## Viewing and downloading history

Each entry records the org, feature, object(s), operation, status, and record counts. From the
detail view you can preview or download:

- **Input Data** — the records that were loaded (for file-based loads)
- **Request Payload** — exactly what was submitted to Salesforce
- **Results** — per-record success/failure, including error messages

Downloads are regular CSV/JSON files, so you can re-load them or archive them anywhere you like.

## Retention, pinning, and limits

History is cleaned up automatically: the oldest entries are removed as new ones are saved.
**Pin** any entry to exclude it from automatic cleanup.

Limits depend on your plan — free plans keep your **15 most recent entries**, while paid plans
(and the desktop app and browser extension) keep unlimited entries for up to a full year.

## Settings

From the **Settings** page you can:

- Turn Data History off entirely (a per-load "don't save" option is also available on each load screen)
- Adjust how long history is kept (paid plans)
- Choose where history is stored — including a real folder on your computer (Chrome/Edge and the
  desktop app), where files are visible, backed up, and survive clearing browser data
- See how much storage your history is using
- Delete all saved history from the device

## Notes

- When Jetstream runs inside Salesforce (canvas), history is stored per Salesforce domain and is
  more likely to be cleared by the browser — use the web or desktop app for a durable history.
- History is per-device: your history on one computer is not visible on another.
